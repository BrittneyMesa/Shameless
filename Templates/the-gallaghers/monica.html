Monica Gallagher

Monica Gallagher (Chloe Webb) is Frank's ex-wife and the mother of the Gallagher brood; she has bipolar disorder. She feels terrible for abandoning her children but can never sustain her love and concern for them for very long before succumbing to drugs, selfishness, and depression.
