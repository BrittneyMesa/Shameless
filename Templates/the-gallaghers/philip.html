Philip "Lip" Gallagher

Phillip "Lip" Gallagher (Jeremy Allen White), born March 29, 1994, is the second Gallagher child and the most academically minded. He was a straight-A student throughout high school, and used his intelligence in various money-making schemes, such as running an ice cream truck that also sold beer and marijuana, or taking the SATs for fellow students (often scoring a 2400, a perfect score) for a fee. Despite his above-average intelligence, his personal life behavior is the opposite; he smokes cigarettes and marijuana, drinks alcohol, pursues casual sexual encounters with any girl, and even steals vehicles.
